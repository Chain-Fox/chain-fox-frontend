Chain Fox Security Policy

At Chain Fox, we take security seriously. This document outlines our security policy and provides information for security researchers who wish to report vulnerabilities.

Reporting Security Issues

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability in our website, services, or products, we encourage you to notify us. We welcome working with skilled security researchers to resolve the issue promptly and protect our users.

Please email your findings to security@chain-fox.com. We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours and strive to send you regular updates about our progress.

Disclosure Policy

  • Please provide detailed reports with reproducible steps. Include the version number of the vulnerable component if applicable.
  • We ask that you give us a reasonable time to investigate and address any findings before public disclosure.
  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, and interruption or degradation of our services.

Security Measures

Chain Fox implements various security measures to protect our users and their data:

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure development practices and code reviews
  • Implementation of industry-standard security headers and protocols
  • Regular updates and patches to address potential vulnerabilities
  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest

Scope

This security policy applies to all Chain Fox services, websites, and applications.
